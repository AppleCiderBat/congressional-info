My Area's Representative

Denny Heck

Democrat

Mr. Heck serves the residents of the 10th Congressional District of Washington. The 10th Congressional District was created by the Washington State Redistricting Commission in 2011. The 10th Congressional District is a brand new Congressional district that Washington gained as a result of population growth last decade. The 10th Congressional District contains most of Pierce County, nearly all of Thurston County and a portion of Mason County. Cities and communities within the 10th Congressional District include: Lakewood, Olympia, Lacey, Tacoma's Eastside, Puyallup, University Place, Shelton, Tumwater, Tenino, Rainier, Yelm, Roy, McKenna, DuPont, Steilacoom, Fircrest, Fife, Sumner, Edgewood, Parkland, Spanaway, Summit-Waller, Midland, Anderson Island, Boston Harbor and parts of Elk Plain, Graham, Frederickson, South Hill and Rochester. The 10th Congressional District also contains all of Joint Base Lewis-McChord except the Yakima Training Center.

Congressman Heck serves on the House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence, the committee in charge of oversight of the seventeen elements of the U.S. Government's Intelligence Community and the Military Intelligence Program. Congressman Heck is also the only Member of Congress from the Pacific Northwest that serves on the House Financial Services Committee. He is a member of the Financial Institutions and Consumer Credit Subcommittee, and the Monetary Policy and Trade Subcommittee.
